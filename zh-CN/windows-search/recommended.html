How to remove recommended on Windows 10

Unfortunately, you're not able to disable it directly.

But you can let the recommended section replaced by recent.

Unless you remove all the recents, you won't see the recommended again.

Just keep at least one recent-used app, it looks much more lovely than a whole list of chaos, right?

Steps as follows:

  1. Win+I
  2. Search
  3. enable Search history on this device
